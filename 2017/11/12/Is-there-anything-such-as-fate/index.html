Is there anything such as fate?

2017-11-12 • 标签: Life

I find it’s hard to answer this in English. So I went to search online in Asian language because I have the same question before.

First, I search the word fate in Chinese “命运”. There are a lot of explanation about this word so I will explain it in short sentences. Fate in Chinese can be separated into two words in Chinese “命” and “运”. “命” means the characteristics that you have when you were born, such as your personality, your look. “运” means the things that will happen in your life, such as your work, your first love, etc. “运” cannot be changed no matter how hard you tried.

So according to your question, yes. You will find that no matter you tried, something will still remain the same. Such as the death of your lovers, parents. These are the things include in “运”.

However, if you switch two characters in the word “命运”, you will get another word that resembles fate: “运命”.

This is a word slightly different than the original one. “运” this character means individually operate, run. “命” is the same. Connect them together, you will find out that “运命” means that how you operate, how you run your own life.

Different than “命运”, you can control your own life if you believing “运命”.

Surprisingly, this two Chinese words both mean fate. So if you want to know whether there is fate or not, choose your belief.
