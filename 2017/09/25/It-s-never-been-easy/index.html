It's never been easy...

2017-09-25 • 标签: Life

Luck isn’t my best friend.
Plus the motto from my parents,
I have trained myself to be stronger and stronger.
I remind myself every time I got a mission.
Remind myself that I’m alone
that I need to rely on myself than other.
But being alone is hard.
You take everything
every responsibility with you.
In other words,
you put the whole world on your shoulder.
This is hard.
I have already trained myself to be strong.
To have a hard shell.
To be the one that other can rely on you.
This is hard.
Sometimes I murmur,
I can’t withstand no more.
But I can’t express it out.
Because this shell have already build an image of superman.
Soft and weak is not allowed to be shown on a superman.
So I take it by myself.
Alone.
I’m not a man who is good at expressing feelings.
I just express it.
Directly.
Sometimes is hard to let it flow out.
Some of my friends say that I’m easy to get angry.
I’m hard to get alone.
That’s the side effect of this shell.
There’s also friends say that I got perseverance.
Because the only thing that supports me,
is my belief.
Sometimes it’s strong.
Sometimes it’s as brittle as a mirror.
Nobody came and said:
It’s fine
Good try
I had carried, have carried, was carrying, am carrying, will carry, would carry all the responsibility, all the aftermath.
I wish I can have somebody that I can rely on.
Because this is too heavy.
Too hard.
